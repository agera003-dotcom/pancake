All about Pancakes!

Here is everything about Pancakes!

Pancakes are a breakfast delicacy that originated in ancient Greece and Rome. They originally were made to use up things like butter and eggs.

Pancakes can be topped with the following:

  1. Berries
  2. Syrup
  3. Butter
  4. Whipped Cream
  5. Chocolate Chips/Nutella

Below is a pancake recipe for delicious pancakes:

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Optional: A sprinkle of salt to add more flavor
  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Heat the pan.
  3. Pour batter.
  4. Flip when bubbles appear.
  5. Serve with toppings.
